# Rhovand

Beyond the Gray Peaks, where the land unfurls into the endless sea of grass known as the Maned Expanse, civilization falters. The Rhovand hold dominion there, nomadic hordes, untamed and unyielding. To call them mere savages would be a mistake, for though their ways are brutal, their numbers alone make them a force to be reckoned with. No lord has tamed those fields, no banner flies unchallenged. The Maned Expanse belongs to the Rhovand, and none who enter leave unchanged, if they leave at all.

# Valar

To the north stretch the Frozen Wastes, a land where the cold bites deep and the weak are swallowed whole. No kingdom lays claim to that desolation, for no city could stand against the unrelenting frost. Only the hardiest endure, and none more so than the Valar. Born to the hunt, they carve their existence from the flesh of the land, their strength and cunning honed by necessity. A single Valar is worth ten men, or so the stories go, though any who have faced one in battle would argue the tale does not do them justice.
