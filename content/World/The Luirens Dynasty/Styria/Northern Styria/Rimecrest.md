The northernmost town is Rimecrest, a frozen stronghold marking the edge of the Whispering Hills, locked in eternal ice. Beyond Rimecrest, only scattered mining settlements remain, where the riches of the Gray Peaks are unearthed and flow into the province. These settlements rely on Rimecrest's industry, as much of the extracted ore is forged into tools essential for their operations. Skilled craftsmen call this frozen town home, their expertise fuelling the province's relentless pursuit of wealth and survival. For a decade now, House Rowe has held dominion over Rimecrest, rising from the embers of House Wells's disgrace. Their grip is firm but fair, their rule bolstered by the goodwill of the townsfolk. None more so than Lady Amelia, whose keen eye and measured hand have earned her the people's favour. She walks among them, not as some distant noble, but as one who understands their toils. Blacksmiths, masons, and weavers alike find fortune under her patronage, their crafts flourishing in her stead. In Rimecrest, her name carries weight, not by fear, but by respect.
