The Luirens Dynasty is a fragile empire, a patchwork of restless provinces constantly on the brink of rebellion. At its helm stands Artholas Luirens, the "Iron Hand," a ruler whose might and righteousness serve as both shield and sword against the chaos threatening his dominion. The nation is ruled by an oligarchy, a council of high nobles, each commanding their own territory with near-autonomous authority. However, the people of Luirens were not always united. Centuries ago, the Therlands consisted of multiple nations, each racing for resources and dominance. Callace Luirens, the head of his dynasty at the time, brought his final enemy, Styria, to its knees. With that victory, he declared himself Emperor, the rightful ruler of his dynasty and all of the Therlands.
