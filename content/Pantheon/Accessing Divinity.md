For those wanting to access the powers granted by divinity, they need to align themselves with the concept the particular divinity represents. For example, to be given powers from the Daemon of Wrath, the individual must be wrathful, and act accordingly to that trait. It is not enough to only act in such ways, but they need to stand out to even be considered, their faith unwavering and near fanatical.

Heroic stories often circulate about people with strong faith discovering new found powers in times of need. These powers surface in heightened emotional situations.

As quick as these abilities come, they can go too. Those losing faith, belief or practice in these concepts may experience their powers waver, or fully gone.

Now, not all with strong faith gain these powers, there seems to be no pattern to this phenomena, if anything this is normal. Only a select few get to gain divine powers with no recognised pattern, the church says those who do are closer to the Maker itself.
