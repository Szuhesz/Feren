When the last light dims and shadows feast upon the land, a lone flame shall rise from the ashes of despair. Blood of kings and sorrow of ages, the Reithalan shall stand where all others fall. With blade unbroken and heart unyielding, they shall walk the path of fire and silence the weeping of their kin. In the hour of reckoning, when the black tide swallows the earth, the Reithalan will strike, and darkness shall tremble. The chains of the forsaken will shatter, and the lost shall find their way home.
