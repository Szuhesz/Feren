The winds carry whispers of doom, and the roots tremble with the coming storm. The skies will dim, their light devoured by a hungering shadow, and the land will weep beneath the weight of horrors long forgotten. From the deep places, they will rise, gnawing at the bones of the earth, feasting upon flesh and spirit alike. The rivers will blacken, the beasts will howl in terror, and the voices of the Ancestors will fade into silence. The Rhovand will be hunted, scattered like embers upon a cold wind, their fire fading in the endless dark.

But the fire is never truly lost. From the embers of despair, a flame will rise, a spirit born of the old ways, of wrath and renewal. Reithalan shall walk the land, their fire unchained, their soul burning brighter than the dying stars. The darkness will recoil at their coming, the Spirits will stir at their call, and the Rhovand will gather once more. No longer prey, no longer broken, they will march as one, their hearts alight with the fury of the Ancestors.

Through storm and blood, through ruin and shadow, they will follow The Fireborn, their steps forging a path to a world untouched by terror. The horrors will be driven to the void, their whispers silenced, their presence erased from the land. When the last shadow crumbles, when the sky shines free, the Rhovand will stand upon sacred ground, a land reclaimed, a world reborn. The spirits will walk beside them, the fire will never fade, and the darkness will never return.
