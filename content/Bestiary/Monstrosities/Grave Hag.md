*Cremation, now that's one thing we could learn from the Rhovand. Burying bodies out in the fields, why, it's like laying out a welcome mat for monsters! Best case scenario, some ghouls will sniff them out, eat their fill and be on their ghoulish way. But if, Angels forbid, a grave hag takes to feeding at your cemetery, you'll have no end of trouble.*

-Professor Marcus Slater
***

Few monsters' names fit as well as the grave hags'. As one might guess, these creatures resemble aged, deformed women and loiter near graveyards and battlefields. Grave hags feed on human corpses and in particular on the rotten marrow which they slurp from human bones using their long, prehensile tongues. Once a hag has devoured all corpses within reach, she turns to killing men and burying them in the cemetery as she waits for them to decompose. They are territorial and aggressive when provoked, using their great speed to rapidly close on prey.
