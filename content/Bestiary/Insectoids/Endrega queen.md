Not much is known about these insectoids, yet their existence is indisputable. Surrounded by their [[Endrega worker|workers]] and [[Endrega warrior|warriors]], the queens are assumed to have one purpose only. Produce the colony's eggs.

Their loyal companions further hint at their existence, as the further into these colonies people found themselves, the fiercer the resistance they put up was.
