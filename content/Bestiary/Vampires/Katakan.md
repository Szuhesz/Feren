*When fighting a katakan one must pay particular attention to its shadow, often the only way to know its location.

-Excerpt from: Lothar's Guide to Survival
***

Katakans are the embodiments of human fear. They hide in the shadows. They feed on blood. They resemble enormous bats, though with long fangs and even longer talons. And, as if that weren't terror enough, they can turn invisible, waiting unseen while dread of their unpreventable attack overwhelms their victim.

Unfortunately, the legends are wrong and the sun's rays do not turn katakans to ash. They are vulnerable to sunlight, however, specifically when the sun stands at its zenith.

Katakans are a vampire race found in the colder regions.

In natural form, katakans have many bat-like traits albeit somewhat larger and monstrous. They have long claws that continue to grow throughout their lives. They are also capable of disguising themselves and acting as normal humans.
