*Common people believe that fleders are dead heretics who turned into vampires. Being vampires, they attack sleeping people and drink their blood. According to peasants, a man bitten by a fleder becomes a fleder himself. This is nonsense, obviously.*

-Reyna Gardson
***

Fleders are lesser vampires, but this description can be deceiving, for lesser does not mean weak and stupid. True, they are primitive and bear a closer relation to animals than to humans, but when they drop on their prey from above, they can be just as dangerous as their noble cousins, the [[Bruxa|bruxae]]. Fleders as [[Ekimma|ekimmaras]] are especially dangerous foes due to their ability to regenerate.
