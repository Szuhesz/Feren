*I regret to inform Your Grace that Your Grace's son fell while hunting a fiend. He died on the spot, along with his squire, his guide, the beaters, his peasant entourage and his hounds.*

-Last words of Andres Ross, Master of the Hunt
***

The beast resembles a deer but much larger. Fiends also have a third eye which they use to hypnotize their opponents.

Fiends are walking mountains of muscle capped with horned, tooth-filled heads. They live in thick forests, swamps and bogs. When possible they avoid humans, but when not possible, they kill them, and without much difficulty.

Their size alone makes fiends extremely dangerous, one blow from their powerful paws can kill a knight along with it's fully armored mount. If that weren't trouble enough, fiends need not rely on their strength and stamina alone with fighting. They can also call on a more refined weapon: the third eye located in the center of their forehead, which they use to draw their prey into a state of hypnosis. During these times their victim does not see anything beyond this single burning eye, the last thing they see before their death.
