It is confirmed, the curse of vampirism and therianthropism are not exclusive. Rare, as it is speculated the host must be strong in both physique and mind to not be torn apart.

A fzeg is a fusion of a vampire and werewolf. The hybrid possesses a combination of abilities more powerful than either. A fzeg's howl can shake the resolve of a seasoned monster hunter.

To blend in and gain the trust of its prey, a fzeg disguises itself as a humanoid. When a kill is imminent, though, a fzeg moves with great speed to feed in insatiable bloodlust.

Death is a welcome option, for its bite may curse its foes with its own afflictions, of which it has a handful of.
