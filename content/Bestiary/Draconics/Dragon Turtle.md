***

Dragon turtles are among the most fearsome creatures of the oceans. As large and voracious as its extinct kin, a dragon turtle strikes with its deadly jaws, steaming breath, and crushing tail.

A dragon turtle's rough shell is the same dark green color as the deep water where this monster dwells. Silver highlights lining the shell resemble light dancing on open water, and a surfacing dragon turtle is sometimes mistaken for the reflection of the sun or moon on the waves.

Scholars still debate over the true identity of these creatures' ancestors, but all evidence seems to point toward one conclusion. This rare breed is the closest thing that remains of the legendary specie known as the dragons.
