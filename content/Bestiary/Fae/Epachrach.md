*Beware the epachrach, an enormous fae tusker. They make giant boars seem friendly and warm.*

-A kind hermit
***

Epachrachs are strange creatures in appearance. A mix of elephant and gorilla, with their tusks seemingly made of sturdy tree branches. Their eyes glow with a constant orange, warning any and all who happen to witness them.

An epachrach is a creature of fae fury and might. Empowered by the strength of ancient oaks, it crashes through the forest, seeking those who defile places of life. It can abruptly manifest, like a sudden storm of woody skin and twisting tusks, bringing pain and death.

They seem like forces of uncontrolled rage, but their wrath has purpose. Each eprachrach, or a herd of such creatures, guards a place of life. So, guardianship describes their nature quite adequately, often producing strange tales, if one isn't a threat. At times where they do, tales such as those are few, if any at all.
