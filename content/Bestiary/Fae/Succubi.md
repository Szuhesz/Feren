A succubus (female) or incubus (male) is a creature, sometimes considered "a monster", looking like a highly attractive human being seducing real humans, first appearing in dreams from time to time, then physically, and is interested only in adults. Neither succubi nor incubi are particularly violent, though they draw energy from the men and women to sustain themselves, often until the point of exhaustion or death of the victim.

Succubi usually can be found near human settlement, including small villages and populous cities. They prowl at night, though when stricken by serious need they will leave their lairs during the day as well. They shower their affections on men as well as women, the young as well as the old, the ugly as well as the beautiful. Some of them are particularly fond of pastors and other holy men, whose seduction they treat as a sort of game.
