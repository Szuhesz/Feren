*If you go into the woods alone, you ought to go prepared. Things that creep on the forest floor might insist you tread lightly, or not at all.*

-A kind hermit
***

Their sharp beak and claws pose a real threat to anyone foolish enough to approach. Though, perhaps the best advice one can follow is to never wander too far off the path, as outrunning them in this environment is impossible.

Lurking in the deepest reaches of forests, braerogs represent unity and survival. These creatures fiercely protect the vestiges of woodlands unsullied by humanity.

Braerogs are one with the forests that surround them and travel through dense parts with ease. They also have a penchant for mischief. A braerog sighting was once a sure sign that a more powerful guardian of the forest was nearby. Without the oversight of such a master, braerogs act unchecked.
