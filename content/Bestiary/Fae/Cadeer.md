*Folks say they were once humans. An old forest being cursed them for being too greedy for magic. Not sure I believe that, but explains the glint in the eyes.*

-Old Frelick
***

Cadeers look like deer, but they have the tail of a snake and black, featureless eyes. They're not only sapient, but highly intelligent. Cadeers also have an affinity to the arcane, as they seem to seek the Awakened out, who wander in their territory.

Legends say they are ought not to be trusted. Often times cadeers will seek partners to seek and find magic. Unfortunately, one most likely won't receive a share in the findings, as they are selfish and greedy creatures.

Their antlers to this day remain a mystery. Resilient and conductive when it comes to magic.
