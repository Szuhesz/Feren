*Marras are literally the stuff of nightmares.*

-Shawn Edsel
***

Tiny creatures with a goat's horn. Fur and wrinkles cover their bodies, nails overgrown and an eye glowing yellow in the dark. A devious smile often accompanies their appearance.

These nocturnal visitors sit on a sleeping victim's chest, drawing out personal fears and driving poor souls to panic before suffocating them.

Those who have survived a marra attack testified that when the creature came to visit, the victim felt a heavy weight on their chest before losing the ability to move. Survivor accounts also report the marra can shapechange, often becoming moths. In this form, a marra can enter rooms through keyholes or other tiny openings.

Marra steal horses and other mounts, riding off into the twilight in search of victims. The animals are left exhausted and covered in sweat by down.
