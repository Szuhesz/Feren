Dryads, also known as eerie wives by humans, are the nymphs of the woods, concentrated primarily in their forest realms.

Dryads tend to have a slender and small build. They are generally similar to humans and can be mistaken for them in certain situations, and vice versa. Their hair is typically chestnut, green or red and is often dreadlocked.

Their skin tones can be described as similar to humans, though often take on forest-like tints of olive, chestnut, red or green. At some occasions however they may appear entirely green. The exact shade is often unclear due to a habit of painting skin into camouflage patterns by scouts and sentinels, as well as wearing clothes patched up out of their natural surroundings, like leaves and twigs, which help them to blend into the forest. Dryads living in settlements in the heart of the fores tend to wear more fanciful dresses.

Dryads act as guardians of their woodland demesnes. Shy and reclusive, they watch interlopers from the trees. They also seem to have a command of nature, as both vegetation and animals answer to their call.
