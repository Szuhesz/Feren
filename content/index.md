## Excerpt from Feren: A World Tainted by Shadow

"*The world of Feren was not always so faithless, so wretched. Its boundless green fields, sprawling forests, and towering mountain ranges were shaped for us, humans, creations of Divinity itself. 
Without their light however, we are merely shadows of their design. We have lost the morality and values once bestowed upon us, letting corruption seep into our lives like a relentless parasite. Kingdoms clash over trivial grievances, mothers forsake their own flesh, and brothers turn their blades on each other. Have we learned nothing? What have we lost in turning our gaze away, in letting complacency reign? Light... once our guide, now a fading memory in the abyss of our neglect.*"



*Up in the branches, two orange eyes glimmer in the moonlight, smouldering like buried embers. They do not blink. They do not waver. They simply watch, fixed upon you... What a weird little guy...*
